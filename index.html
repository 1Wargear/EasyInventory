Floorplan Navigation

Floor plan navigation enables intuitive navigation through the Invenar. Users only need to click on the room to see all the elements associated with that room.

Floorplan Heatmap

With the heatmaps you can easily get an overview of item distribution and attention requirements of items spread over the whole inventory.

Notifications

Notifications provide information about items that require attention, these are cross-inventory to provide a quick overview.

Multiuser

Share your inventory with other people to get a better overview of the inventory in a club, for example. The authorisation system can also be used in small companies.

Crossplatform

Easy Inventory is cross-platform and can therefore be used natively not only on the web but also on many end devices in the future.
